Siberia, Russia — 1908

The Tunguska Event

In 1908 a 50-60m meteorite hit near Podkamennaya Tunguska River in Siberia, Russia. That hit was so strong that it flattened 80 million trees over 2,000 square km. People in a radius of 1,000km would still hear that sound. The Tunguska explosion is equivalent to 1,000 Hiroshima bombs.

Russian Banya

Banya is a high humidity steam bath, often built of wood. People would visit for cleansing, health, and community.

💊

HEALTH

People were going to that bath for health — the heat and steam were seen as cleansing and healing.

🌿

DETOX

People were going to that bath for detoxification, using whisks to cleanse themselves.

🤝

SOCIAL

People were going to that bath for socialization — it was a shared communal tradition.
